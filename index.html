About the Classes

Boclaire's wellness classes integrate practices that support physical strength, emotional balance, focus, and self-awareness.

  • Creative somatic movement for emotional health
  • Fun neuro exercises for brain health
  • Yoga for flexibility of the mind and body
  • Meditation and breathing for calm minds
  • Mindful living and healthy eating practices
  • Therapeutic sound and simple pressure points

Your Instructor

Boclaire Vaughn

Boclaire is a Natural Wellness Practitioner certified in Child Development, Yoga, and Acupressure Therapy. She is the author of Children's Meditation Stories and the founder of Sierra Yoga Center and the True Wellness Movement.

She has facilitated Kids' Yoga, Mindful Parenting, and Growing Mindful Minds workshops for many years as a contract instructor with the Tuolumne County Recreation Department.
