About the Founder

Boclaire

Natural Wellness Practitioner & Community Educator

Boclaire has taught for many years in Tuolumne County, offering yoga, movement, and mindfulness-based education to children, families, and the community.

She is the author of Children's Meditation Stories and is currently available for school enrichment programs and homeschool co-ops.

Certified InChild Development
Certified InYoga
Certified InAcupressure Therapy
AuthorChildren's Meditation Stories
Sierra Yoga Center Mindful Mom True Wellness Movement

She has facilitated kids yoga & movement classes, Mindful Parenting workshops, and Growing Mindful Minds workshops. Boclaire has also served as a contract instructor with the Tuolumne County Recreation Department.
